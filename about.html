About

SBU Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine

Amplifying graduate voices at the intersection of science, art, and public engagement

The Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine at Stony Brook University is an official student organization founded in 2023. We serve as a platform where graduate students can share their academic research with their peers, faculty, and the wider community.

The magazine provides an opportunity for graduate students to showcase their talents, explore new topics, and engage in interdisciplinary discussions. With a focus on intellectual curiosity and community building, we create spaces for graduate students to engage and collaborate through our publications and events.

Each year, we publish a magazine featuring research highlights, creative work, photography, poetry, and visual storytelling. We also host events including our annual B.R.I.D.G.E.S. conference, social gatherings, and our radio show Tech & Friends on WUSB 90.1 FM.

Our Mission

We are a platform for interdisciplinary conversation: publishing creative, critical, and experimental work that connects research to wider communities and celebrates the diversity of graduate scholarship.
