About

SBU Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine

Amplifying graduate voices at the intersection of science, art, and public engagement

The Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine at Stony Brook University is an official student organization. We serve as a platform where graduate students can share their academic research with their peers, faculty, and the wider community.

The magazine provides an opportunity for graduate students to showcase their talents, explore new topics, and engage in interdisciplinary discussions. With a focus on intellectual curiosity and community building, graduate students are given the opportunity to engage and collaborate with others through our events.

Our Mission

We are a platform for interdisciplinary conversation: publishing creative, critical, and experimental work that connects research to wider communities. Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic research and public understanding, making graduate work accessible and engaging for all.

What We Do

Each year, we publish a magazine featuring research highlights, creative work, photography, poetry, and visual storytelling from Stony Brook's graduate community. We also host events including our annual B.R.I.D.G.E.S. conference, workshops, and social gatherings that bring graduate students together across departments.
