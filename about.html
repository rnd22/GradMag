About GradMag

The Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine at Stony Brook University is an official student organization. We serve as a platform where graduate students can share their academic research with their peers, faculty, and the wider community.

The magazine provides an opportunity for graduate students to showcase their talents, explore new topics, and engage in interdisciplinary discussions.

Our Mission

We are a platform for interdisciplinary conversation: publishing creative, critical, and experimental work that connects research to wider communities.

What We Do

Each year, we publish a magazine featuring research highlights, creative work, photography, poetry, and visual storytelling. We also host events including our annual B.R.I.D.G.E.S. conference and social gatherings.

What was Grad Mag up to in 2025?

  • Hosted our first conference, B.R.I.D.G.E.S., on January 30th with over 200 attendees
  • Attended ProCon25 and was nominated for the 2025 College Media Association David L. Adams NYC Apple Award
  • Published The Four Seasons in April
  • Received the Collaborative Program Award, Media Programming Award, and Most Innovative Use of Media
  • Named Best Club of the Year by the GSO Executive Committee
  • Tied for Best Cover at the Student Media Council Awards
  • President Ava Nederlander named Executive Board Member of the Year
  • Leadership awards for Veronica Borracci, Jennifer L. O'Connor, and Ava Nederlander
