What We Publish

We accept short-form and long-form articles, poetry, photography, and art from Stony Brook graduate students. Each spring we release a new edition celebrating the creativity and scholarship of our graduate community.

Typical Timeline:

  • September/October: Call for abstracts, artists, and poetry go out
  • October: Priority submission deadline
  • January/February: Final submission deadline
  • April: Magazine goes to print
  • May: Magazine Release Celebration

For questions, please reach out via email at sbugradmag@stonybrook.edu.
