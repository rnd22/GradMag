What We Publish

Our magazines are released in April/May of each year. We plan to follow a similar production schedule for our 2026 edition. We accept short-form and long-form articles, poetry, photography, art. For questions, please reach out via email at sbugradmag@stonybrook.edu.

Read our 2025 edition

Our Spring 2025 magazine was just released this past April, be sure to check it out on our Publications page.

You can contact us at sbugradmag@stonybrook.edu with any questions you might have.
