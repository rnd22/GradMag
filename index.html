About GradMag

The Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine at Stony Brook University is an official student organization. We serve as a platform where graduate students can share their academic research with their peers, faculty, and the wider community. The magazine provides an opportunity for graduate students to showcase their talents, explore new topics, and engage in interdisciplinary discussions. With a focus on intellectual curiosity and community building, graduate students are given the opportunity to engage and collaborate with others through our events.

The Stony Brook Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine hosted our first conference, B.R.I.D.G.E.S, in January of 2025. For more information about the conference, see our B.R.I.D.G.E.S page.

What has Grad Mag been up to this year?

  • Hosted our first conference, B.R.I.D.G.E.S., on January 30th with over 200 attendees
  • Attended ProCon25 in February and was nominated for the 2025 College Media Association David L. Adams NYC Apple Award
  • Published the second edition of our magazine, The Four Seasons, in April
  • Received the Collaborative Program Award at the Jerrold L. Stein Student Life Awards, plus the Media Programming Award and Most Innovative Use of Media at the Annual Student Media Council Awards for our B.R.I.D.G.E.S. conference
  • Recognized by the GSO Executive Committee as the Best Club of the Year
  • Tied for Best Cover at the Annual Student Media Council Awards
  • Our president, Ava Nederlander, was named Executive Board Member of the Year at the Jerrold L. Stein Student Life Awards and won the Best Club Leadership Award
  • Our secretary, Veronica Borracci; Vice President, Jennifer L. O’Connor; and President, Ava Nederlander, received the Student Media Council Leadership Award
