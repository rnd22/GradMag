The Graduate Arts & Sciences Magazine at Stony Brook University is an official student organization. We serve as a platform where graduate students can share their academic research with peers, faculty, and the wider community.

The magazine provides an opportunity for graduate students to showcase talents, explore new topics, and engage in interdisciplinary discussion. With a focus on intellectual curiosity and community building, graduate students are given the opportunity to engage and collaborate with others through our events.
